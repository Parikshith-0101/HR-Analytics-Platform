How Risk Is Evaluated

The prediction model analyzes multiple employee data points to assess retention likelihood.

  • Satisfaction Metrics
    Evaluates job, environment, and work-life balance scores.
  • Compensation Data
    Analyzes salary level, recent hikes, and stock options.
  • Career Progression
    Reviews time since last promotion and manager tenure.
  • Workload Patterns
    Factors in overtime requirements and job involvement.
