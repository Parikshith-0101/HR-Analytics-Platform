Key Attrition Insights

Crucial patterns identified by our machine learning model across the organization.

  • Overtime Correlation: Employees with frequent overtime show significantly increased attrition probability compared to the baseline.
  • Work-Life Balance: Low work-life balance strongly correlates with employee exits, particularly when combined with long commute times.
  • Career Stagnation: Long promotion gaps (3+ years) are associated with higher resignation risk among mid-level employees.
  • Compensation: Monthly income levels heavily influence retention, specifically when benchmarked against industry averages.
